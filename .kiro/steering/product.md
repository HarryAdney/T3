# Product Overview

Thoralby Through Time is a heritage website dedicated to preserving and sharing the rich history of Thoralby and the surrounding Bishopdale area in North Yorkshire, England. The site serves as a digital archive and community resource for local historical content.

## Core Features

- **Historical Archive**: Photographs, documents, maps, and records
- **Township Information**: Detailed pages for Bishopdale, Thoralby, Burton-cum-Walden, and Newbiggin
- **People & Families**: Directory of individuals who shaped local history
- **Timeline**: Chronological journey through centuries of events
- **Content Management**: Admin interface for authenticated users to manage content
- **Search & Browse**: Functionality to explore historical materials

## Design Philosophy

Heritage-focused design with warm, earthy color palette (sage, parchment, stone tones), serif typography for historical character, and subtle animations for modern user experience. Fully responsive across all devices.

## Target Users

- Local community members and historians
- Researchers and genealogists
- Visitors interested in Yorkshire Dales history
- Content contributors and administrators
